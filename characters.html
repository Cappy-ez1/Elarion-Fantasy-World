The Great Figureheads

Imagine the heavy, golden ledgers kept by the Imperial Archivists, tracking the blood, the spellcraft, and the ghosts of the five realms.

I. Frostbound Dominion Ledger

The Mountain Throne
THE LATE MONARCHS King Vane Ironjaw & Queen Sifra Species: Deep Dwarf / Goliath hybrid

Famous for commissioning the Great Geothermal Piston that saved the city from the 1100 AC Century Freeze.

Born: 980 AC Died: 1190 AC (Chasm collapse)
PRESENT SOVEREIGNS King Torvald II & Queen Runa Species: Pure Deep Dwarf

Currently ruling via martial law. Torvald wears a pressurized brass lung after an assassination attempt.

Born: 1088 AC / 1102 AC (Living)
PROPHESIED HEIR Crown Princess Freya Steam-Song Species: Dwarf

The first royal born with sight in four generations; prophesied to lead the Dwarves back to the sun.

Born: 1228 AC (Living)
STATE MAGUS Master Cryo-Weaver Kori Species: Deep Dwarf

Famous for casting 'Absolute Zero' to instantly freeze an erupting subterranean magma pocket in 1210 AC.

Born: 1150 AC (Living)
HISTORICAL ICON High Mechanist Thorgar Species: Deep Dwarf

Invented the first titanium 'Sky-Tether' link used to tie down Skyport Aetherhold.

Born: 42 AC Died: 310 AC (Old age)
MODERN CELEBRITY Lyra Vane, The Zephyr Species: Aether-Touched

The undisputed, four-time champion of the dangerous Cloud-Galleon Slalom races.

Born: 1215 AC (Living)

The Obsidian Spire

Authors of the 'Seven Corporate Laws of Thermomancy'. They hold the realm's spell-patents in an iron grip.

Born: 1040 AC / 1055 AC (Living)

Born with pure white plasma for hair; she successfully cast her first Tier-4 Fireball at age four.

Born: 1225 AC (Living)
SUPREME ARCH-MAGE Azimuth The Unburnt Species: Fire Genasi

The most feared battle-mage alive; permanently resides inside the superheated plasma chamber of the Shard-Forge.

Born: 1111 AC (Living)
HISTORICAL LEGEND Morwen The Glass-Maker Species: High Elf

Wrote the original 'Standard Universal Model of the Aether' on a single massive slab of volcanic glass.

Born: 12 BCE Died: 450 AC (Natural)

Invented the 'Pocket Ley-Link', allowing normal citizens to turn on household lights via ambient mana.

The Solar Syndicate

Can sweat pure, drinkable condensation when subjected to extreme duress; viewed as a living holy relic.

Legend says he won the coordinates to the Oasis of Zahar in a game of loaded dice against a starving God.

Born: 105 BCE Died: 12 AC (Dehydration)

A legendary cat-burglar who successfully stole the Solarch's left golden sandal right off his foot during a banquet.
