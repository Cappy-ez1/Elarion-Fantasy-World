GEOPOLITICAL SURVEY

The Five Sovereign Crowns

Imagine a shattered globe held together only by the fragile tension between five vastly different philosophies of survival.

THE NORTHERN CRAGS

Frostbound Dominion

Capital: Frostpeak

Imagine a brutalist, subterranean empire built inside hollowed-out glaciers. They view the surface world as soft and doomed, placing their faith entirely in the rhythm of the trip-hammer.

THE STRENGTHImpenetrable natural mountain ramparts & infinite iron reserves.
THE GREAT NEEDArable topsoil, citrus (scurvy protection), and heavy wool.
COMMERCIAL EXPORTRunesteel Ingots & Cryo-Gems
DRIVING TECHNOLOGYGeothermal Cryo-Steam Engines
THE VOLCANIC CALDERA

Emberfall Magocracy

Capital: Diamondreach

Imagine a society where your societal rank is dictated purely by the decibels of your incantations. Ruled by corporate spell-syndicates who patent the very laws of physics.

THE STRENGTHAbsolute, unchallenged battlefield air & artillery superiority.
THE GREAT NEEDStable, non-shifting foundation stone & liquid coolants.
COMMERCIAL EXPORTData-Encoded Spell Prisms
DRIVING TECHNOLOGYHard-Light Arcane Weaving
THE ENDLESS WASTES

Golden Dunes Confederacy

Capital: Sunspire

Imagine an ultra-capitalist web of merchant princes who bought their independence with water. They treat the scorching desert sun not as an enemy, but as an infinite furnace to be focused.

THE STRENGTHMonopoly on subterranean deep fresh water aquifers.
THE GREAT NEEDHeavy structural hardwoods and shade-yielding flora.
COMMERCIAL EXPORTSolar-Forged Glass & Bullion
DRIVING TECHNOLOGYThermal-Hydraulic Sun-Towers
THE MONSOON STRAITS

Tempest Maritime Union

Capital: Stormhold

Imagine a floating meritocracy of lashed galleons and sea-fortresses that perpetually rides the outer hurricane wall. To them, land-dwellers are merely people waiting to drown.

THE STRENGTHUnrivaled naval logistics & perfect storm prediction.
THE GREAT NEEDRaw non-corroding iron ore (salt spray ruins theirs).
COMMERCIAL EXPORTRefined Leviathan Oil & Pearls
DRIVING TECHNOLOGYKinetic Wave-Harnessing Sails
THE PRIMORDIAL TAPROOT

The Root Council (The Heartlands)

Capital: Eternal Greenwood

Imagine an ancient, sentient ecosystem that decided to form a government purely to keep the industrial nations from turning it into paper. They do not build cities; they coax the World Tree's gargantuan roots to open up hollow, living cathedral chambers for them to inhabit.

THE STRENGTHSelf-repairing architecture & infinite sustainable food.
THE GREAT NEEDRefined mineral salts and forged heavy steel blades.
COMMERCIAL EXPORTLiving Iron-Wood & Soul Elixirs
DRIVING TECHNOLOGYAccelerated Hyper-Botany
