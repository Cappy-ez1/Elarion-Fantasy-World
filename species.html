BIOLOGICAL ARCHIVE

The Sentient Species

Imagine a world where human flesh is merely one canvas among stone, bark, static electricity, and sea-foam.

FLORA-SAPIENS

Sylvari (Tree-Elves)

"Skin of polished silver-birch, with eyes that bloom like spring violets when casting."

Native Habitat: Upper Canopies of the Primordial Heartlands.

Global Utility: Able to speak to wooden ship hulls to instantly mend leaks mid-voyage.

LITHIC-SAPIENS

Deep Dwarves

"Squat, wide humanoids born entirely without eyes; their skin resembles cold, pale granite."

Native Habitat: Sub-zero volcanic steam-vents of the Frostpeaks.

Global Utility: They possess a sonar-like "stone-sense", making them the only architects who can hear a mountain's micro-fractures.

ATMOS-SAPIENS

The Aether-Touched

"Humans born at high altitudes whose veins glow through their skin with faint, crawling static."

Native Habitat: The floating piers of Skyport Aetherhold.

Global Utility: They act as living lightning conductors; a single Touch-born can jump-start a dead airship turbine with their bare hands.

PYRO-SAPIENS

Fire Genasi

"Ashen, dark-grey skin topped with hair that is literally a slow, flickering heat-shimmer."

Native Habitat: The Obsidian Caldera of Emberfall.

Global Utility: Immune to ambient heat up to 180°C, they act as the master gem-smelters inside the deadly core of the Shard-Forge.

FAUNA-SAPIENS

Leonin

"Seven-foot-tall bipedal felines covered in dense, short-cropped fur the color of raw desert honey."

Native Habitat: The shifting dunes outside Sunspire.

Global Utility: They possess a biological magnetic internal compass unaffected by the blinding sandstorms that swallow normal caravans.

AQUA-SAPIENS

Lutra (River-Folk)

"Sleek, digitigrade mammalian humanoids with webbed claws and dense, waterproof velvet undercoats."

Native Habitat: The brackish waters of the Root-Tide Delta.

Global Utility: Unmatched hydro-cartographers; the only creatures alive capable of mapping the ever-shifting underwater taproots of the World Tree.

MORTAL-SAPIENS

Humans

"Highly adaptable, ambitious builders whose short lifespans fuel an intense drive for rapid modernization."

Native Habitat: Dominant across Skyport, Sunspire, Stormhold, and Root-Tide.

Global Utility: Unmatched logistical organizers driving thermal-hydraulics, kinetic sails, and civilian ley-links.

ARCANA-SAPIENS

High Elves

"Slender humanoids with crystalline eyes and an ancient affinity for ambient mana resonance."

Native Habitat: Pale spires of Diamondreach and Vestiges ruins.

Global Utility: Foundational arcane codifiers who transcribed the Universal Aether Model onto volcanic glass.

ARBOR-SAPIENS

Treants (Living Oaks)

"Colossal, slow-speaking wooden entities whose bark records the physical growth rings of centuries."

Native Habitat: Deep heartwood groves of Eternal Greenwood.

Global Utility: Sentient ecological guardians allowing their massive roots to open hollow cathedral chambers.

MIRAGE-SAPIENS

Djinn-Blooded

"Mortals carrying the elemental heritage of ancient wish-granters, wreathed in the scent of ozone."

Native Habitat: The grand bazaars of the Oasis of Zahar.

Global Utility: Master mirage-weavers capable of projecting city-wide optical illusions to disorient armies.

PELAGIC-SAPIENS

Tritons

"Amphibious, silver-scaled warriors featuring functional gills and heavy, finned muscular tails."

Native Habitat: Submerged ship-locks beneath Stormhold.

Global Utility: Expert fleet tide-callers able to summon localized 40-foot rogue waves to swallow pirate frigates.

FABRICATED-SAPIENS

Warforged Constructs

"Sentient stone and adamantine laborers inscribed with glowing cyan and pink resonance runes."

Native Habitat: Superheated crystal chambers of the Shard-Forge.

Global Utility: Immune to extreme crystal radiation during raw lightning arc-smelting operations.
