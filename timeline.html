CHRONICLES OF THE REALM

The Great Timeline

Imagine a history written not by the victors, but recorded by the slow, concentric growth rings of the World Tree.

B.C.E. ERA

The Unbroken Age

Before the Fracture, Elarion was a single, super-massive Pangaea ruled by the immortal 'Progenitors'. Magic was not cast; it simply existed as a dense, breathable atmospheric fog.

105 B.C.E.

The Dice Game of Zahar

Kadir The First Merchant successfully wagers his soul in a game of loaded dice against a starving deity, winning the primordial coordinates to Elarion's deep desert oasis.

YEAR 0 A.C.

The Sky-Tear Calamity

An arrogant attempt to pull a passing comet into the atmosphere for its raw mithril shatters the tectonic plates. The Progenitors vanish instantly. The ocean boils, and the lower crust falls into the molten mantle.

342 A.C.

The Seeding of the Taproot

Saint Yvaine drops the Primordial Core into the toxic western ash-pit. Within forty-eight hours, the World Tree grows 4,000 meters into the sky, its roots stitching the cracking continental shelf back together.

450 A.C.

The Volcanic Glass Codex

Morwen The Glass-Maker successfully completes the original transcription of the 'Standard Universal Model of the Aether' onto a massive single slab of cooled obsidian.

890 A.C.

The Aetherial Ascension

Frostbound engineers and human spell-smiths successfully launch the first multi-ton stone landmass into permanent orbit using static-repulsion cells, founding Skyport Aetherhold.

1100 A.C.

The Century Freeze

A devastating sub-zero drop threatens Frostpeak Citadel, thwarted only by King Vane Ironjaw commissioning the Great Geothermal Piston to warm the subterranean glacial vaults.

1244 A.C.

The Present Era

The World Tree's leaves have begun turning a faint, sickly violet at the tips. The Solarch of the Dunes has doubled the price of water, and the Frostpeak pistons are overheating. A second fracture feels imminent.
